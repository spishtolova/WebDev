Tips for the softest cookies

  • For cut-out sugar cookies, chill the dough for at least 1 hour, then roll it out and use cookie cutters to create shapes.

A Sweet Tradition: The Perfect Sugar Cookies

There’s something nostalgic about baking sugar cookies. The moment the dough comes together—soft, buttery, and full of vanilla aroma—it brings back childhood memories of sneaking a taste before rolling it out. Sugar cookies always have a way of making any moment feel special.

Whether you’re baking them for a holiday celebration, a cozy weekend treat, or simply to brighten someone’s day, sugar cookies are a classic favorite that never goes out of style.

These cookies are as versatile as they are delicious. From a simple sprinkle of sugar to intricate icing designs, sugar cookies offer endless opportunities for creativity.

With this simple yet foolproof recipe, you’ll have a batch of perfectly golden sugar cookies ready to share (or keep all to yourself!). They’re not just cookies; they’re memories waiting to be made.

Instructions

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper or silicone baking mats.

In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and baking powder. Set aside. In a large bowl, use a hand or stand mixer to cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy (about 2-3 minutes).

Mix in the egg, vanilla extract, and almond extract (if using) until fully incorporated.

Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing on low speed. If the dough feels too crumbly, add 1 tablespoon of milk at a time until it comes together.

Scoop tablespoon-sized portions of dough and roll them into balls. If desired, roll the balls in colored sugar or sprinkles for decoration. Place them 2 inches apart on the prepared baking sheets.

Bake in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes, or until the edges are set and lightly golden. Be careful not to overbake—sugar cookies should stay soft.

Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 2 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Once cooled, decorate with royal icing or buttercream frosting if desired. Let the frosting set before stacking or storing.

Enjoy your sweet and buttery sugar cookies at any time of the day!

Ingredients:

For the cookies:

  • 2 ¾ cups (350g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 ½ cups (300g) granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp almond extract (optional)
  • 2-3 tbsp milk (if needed for consistency)
Optional for decorating:
  • Colored sugar or sprinkles (for rolling)
  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter, softened
  • Royal icing or buttercream frosting
