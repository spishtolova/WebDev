About Me 🍰✨

Greetings! I'm Stella, a passionate home baker with a love for all things sweet and homemade.


How did I get into baking?

My journey into baking started in my grandmother’s kitchen, where the scent of freshly baked bread and cookies filled the air.

I remember standing on a little wooden stool, watching in awe, teaching me that baking isn’t just about following a recipe.

It’s about love, patience, and a little bit of magic.


Something more about me!

As I grew older, my fascination with flavors and textures deepened. I found myself experimenting with everything

From classic chocolate chip cookies, cakes to all sorts of delicate French pastries.

There’s something so special about transforming simple ingredients into something that brings joy to others.

Whether it’s the warm comfort of a homemade loaf of banana bread or the elegance of a perfectly piped macaron, each creation tells a story.


Why a blog?

This blog is my little corner of the internet, where I share my favorite recipes, baking tips, and the inspiration behind each sweet treat.

Whether you're a seasoned baker or just starting out, I hope my recipes spark creativity in your kitchen and bring a little extra sweetness to your day! 🍪🎂💕
